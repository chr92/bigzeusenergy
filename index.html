BIG ZEUS ENERGY PRESENT:

WEREWOLVES OF LONDON

Masculinity is in crisis. The men's circle is calling and we're heading deep into the forest to find our Big Zeus Energy.

Inspired by the men's movements of the 90s, this physical comedy and clown show will have you questioning everything you know about what it means to be a man. With a mix of fairy tales, dance, and rituals, they will transform man-boys into man-men.

This show promises to be an enlightening experience for audiences of all genders. Described by past audience members as ‘bonkers’.

Nominated: BEST DEBUT SHOW Leicester Comedy Festival
